How does a high efficiency AC save my annual cooling cost?

From the Ontario consumer report, usually a 3 members family would spend a range between $350-$ 450 hydro cost for cooling in summer time. When upgrading old AC (under 10 seers to 14.5 or higher, a saving between $100-$120 annually is possible)

AC saving is based on the total summer days (1498 hrs.) (117 days with June, July, August, and September.) and the sizing criteria set out by American Standard 2.0 Ton furnace NT installed.
